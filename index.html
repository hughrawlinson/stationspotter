ABOUT

Since 1998 the International Space Station has been orbiting earth at an altitude between 205 and 270 miles. The station completes just under 15 orbits per day loaded with up to 6 crew working on a variety of microgravity and space environment research experiments in an array of varying fields. More info can be found on the NASA ISS Mission Page.

Sign up to be notified when the ISS will be coming into view at your location at spotthestation.nasa.gov.
